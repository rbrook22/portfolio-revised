About Me

A brief look into my life

I'm originally from Georgia, but grew up in North Carolina and attended the University of North Carolina at Charlotte where I majored in Criminal Justice. Upon graduation, I worked for LendingTree as a Relationship and Operations Manager before joining the U.S. Navy Submarine Force. After finishing my time in the military and moving into a Customer Relations role with Siemens I decided I was ready for a new challenge. Given my love for all things technology it only seemed natural that I would find my passion in web development.

So I began my new challenge where I attended DigitalCrafts' four-month, full-time immersive program. I graduate from the program in the middle of August where I plan to put my passion to use for a company designing and solving problems.
