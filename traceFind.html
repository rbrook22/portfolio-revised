Trace & Find

Trace & Find Inspiration

Trace & Find originated with the purpose of giving users a platform to search and report items that are lost/found. Users can enter in specific item descriptions, location found, date & time the item was located allowing those individuals searching for their precious item to be reconnected in moments!

A Compelling Experience

The homepage was built around the idea of instantly giving a clear and quick impression about the site and itâ€™s purpose. A clean design with simple coloring to draw the user in about it's purpose. That included introducing the users to an engaging, simple but clean layout for reporting found items.

The mobile version was designed to function on portable devices, allowing the users to search for their lost items from wherever they are. Hoping those good samaritans go online to report those items and connecting those individuals to reunite with their items from wherever they be was the premise behind this application.
