R.A.D. Hiking

R.A.D. Inspiration

R.A.D. Hiking originated from the members of Rick, Aylin, and Delia with the idea of giving users the ability to search for the perfect hike on the go. The site allows hiking enthusiasts and other users the ability to look over features like length, rating, and difficulty level so all ranges of expertise can find the perfect trail to hike. Added features like weather & sunrise/sunset information make it possible that you experience the best conditions on your hike.
